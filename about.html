"Creativity is nothing but a mind set free."

My journey began in 2019 at Tshwane University of Technology (TUT), where I enrolled in a Multimedia program that covered everything from design to development. After five years of rigorous study — including projects, presentations, and creative challenges — I wrapped up my academic journey in October 2024. My final module required real-world experience, which led me to intern at a forward-thinking software company starting April 2024.

What started as an internship quickly grew into something more. My curiosity, commitment, and ability to pick up new tools fast earned me a permanent role — I transitioned from Software Engineering Intern to Frontend Software Engineer in the same year. This moment marked a turning point in my career and solidified my love for building digital interfaces that people love to use.

Outside of my full-time role, I’m a creative freelancer. I’ve worked on a variety of projects, from corporate websites like Mazza Engineering, to full e-commerce systems for brands like ACTS. Whether it’s building with Vue.js or designing eye-catching posters, I enjoy bringing ideas to life — visually and functionally.
