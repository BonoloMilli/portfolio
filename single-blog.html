Mazza Engineering

18,May 2025 website development

Mazza Engineering needed a clean and professional online presence to showcase their services, projects, and contact details. They wanted a corporate design that reflected their brand with clear navigation and responsive design.

I started by gathering their brand materials, analyzing competitor websites, and building wireframes. After approval, I developed the site using HTML, CSS, jQuery, and Bootstrap for fast, responsive pages. I also added basic SEO and smooth scroll navigation.

The design focused on simplicity, clarity, and professionalism. I tested across browsers and devices before final deployment to their hosting provider.
